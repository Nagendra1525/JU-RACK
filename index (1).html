About JECRC University Rack

JECRC University Rack is an online marketplace designed to showcase and monetize student creations, fostering creativity and entrepreneurship.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower students through an online platform that nurtures creativity and collaboration.

Vision: To establish JECRC University Rack as a leading online retail hub for student-designed products.
