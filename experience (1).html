Unique Selling Proposition

The platform provides a marketplace exclusively for JECRC students, fostering creativity, innovation, and economic empowerment.

Product Categories

  • Art & Design
  • Fashion & Textiles
  • Technology & Innovation
  • Handicrafts
  • Photography & Music
  • Educational Materials

Product Procurement & Submission

Students can submit their work through:

  • Online submission portal with images, descriptions, and pricing.
  • Faculty recommendations for promising student work.
  • Workshops and competitions to encourage high-quality submissions.
  • Department partnerships to source diverse products.
  • Quality control and seasonal collection strategies.

Criteria for Product Approval

  • Originality: Must be student-created and plagiarism-free.
  • Quality: Assessed by faculty or industry experts.
  • Relevance: Aligned with customer interest.
  • Compliance: Must follow legal and intellectual property rules.
